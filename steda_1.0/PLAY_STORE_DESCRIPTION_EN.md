Steda – Track your habits, stay consistent, grow together.

Want to build new habits and finally stick with them? Steda turns good intentions into visible progress – clear, motivating and free of distraction. Set up your own habits, check them off every day, and watch your streak grow in beautiful charts.

★ WHY STEDA?
Steda is intentionally simple: four clear sections, big buttons, animated progress indicators and a dark, easy-on-the-eyes design. No ad clutter, no unnecessary features – just what helps you actually reach your goals.

★ YOUR HABITS, YOUR RULES
• Create any habit you like – workouts, reading, drinking water, meditation, studying and more.
• Set a weekly target for each habit (e.g. "4× per week").
• Choose the length of your challenge freely, in weeks.
• Check off every day in a tap: done, not done, or intentionally skipped.

★ PROGRESS THAT MOTIVATES
• A hero dashboard with an animated ring chart shows at a glance how well you're doing this week.
• Animated progress bars and counting-up percentages.
• Calendar-style heatmap: see your whole journey at a glance and spot your patterns.
• Streak display with a flame icon – keep your run alive!

★ WEEK & REFLECTION
• Clear weekly statistics for every habit.
• A reflection area to capture your week and become more mindful.
• Completion rates count only what truly matters – fair and transparent.

★ STRONGER TOGETHER: TRIBE SYNC (optional)
• Join your "Tribe" with a simple group code.
• Leaderboard with a podium (🥇🥈🥉) – compete in a friendly way and motivate each other.
• Built-in group chat to push one another and celebrate wins.
• You decide how many details you share.

★ YOUR DATA BELONGS TO YOU
• Use Steda fully offline – all data stays only on your device.
• Or enable optional online storage to back up and restore your progress across devices.
• No real names, no mandatory email – sign-in is anonymous.
• Export & import your data as a file – a backup in your hands anytime.

★ THOUGHTFUL DESIGN
• Modern, dark UI with fresh accents.
• Smooth animations and haptic touch feedback.
• Reduced to four sections: Overview · Track · Week · Tribe.
• Quick start, no registration needed to begin.

★ WHO IS STEDA FOR?
For everyone who wants to turn small steps into real change: fitness routines, healthier eating, more mindfulness, learning goals, less screen time – or simply the good feeling of staying consistent, day after day.

Download Steda now, create your first habit and start your streak. Your future self will thank you. ✅
